About AskStephen.ai

Where business strategy meets artificial intelligence. AskStephen.ai unites visionary leadership with applied AI to help companies grow intelligently and sustainably.

Stephen E. Wright

Stephen is the author of Pick One Strategy and the creator of the AskStephen.ai framework for executive decision-making. With more than 25 years in business leadership, strategic consulting, and organizational transformation, he helps executives align data, vision, and execution.

As founder of New View Innovation Financial, LLC, Stephen leads the AskStephen.ai Mastermind — a comprehensive business growth program that integrates strategy design, AI insights, and leadership development for $5–15M organizations.

“Great leaders see opportunities others miss — and act with clarity.”

Van Wilson, M.S.

Van is a Data Scientist, MIT graduate, and co-founder of 3A Automation Authority. With 25+ years of experience designing intelligent automation systems for small and mid-sized businesses, he brings a deep understanding of how AI transforms operations and decision-making.

As the AI Systems Director for AskStephen.ai, Van leads the design of advanced tools and algorithms that help executives track metrics, identify growth opportunities, and implement strategies with precision.

“AI isn’t replacing leaders — it’s empowering them to lead with data and confidence.”

Our Mission

To equip business leaders with AI-driven strategy tools that make clarity, execution, and growth measurable.

Through AskStephen.ai, we provide data intelligence, mentorship, and proven frameworks to transform how companies think, plan, and grow. Every engagement — whether through the Mastermind, SaaS, or consulting — is built around one principle: decisions powered by insight lead to sustainable success.
