About AskStephen.ai

AskStephen.ai was founded through a collaboration between New View Innovation Financial, LLC and 3A – AI Automation Authority with one shared mission: to help small-to-mid-sized business owners simplify strategy, automate growth, and rediscover the joy in running their businesses.

Led by business coach and author Stephen Wright, the creator of the Pick One Strategy system, AskStephen.ai combines decades of real-world experience with cutting-edge AI research tools. Together, we deliver clarity, focus, and measurable results for companies generating between $5M–$15M annually.

About Stephen Wright

With over 30 years of experience as a business strategist, fractional CEO, lecturer, and trainer, Stephen has worked with more than 200 successful companies across North America.

Known for his plain-English approach and results-driven coaching style, Stephen helps business owners cut through the chaos and focus on the decisions that matter most.

He co-created the AskStephen.ai platform to make his decades of wisdom accessible through AI automation, enabling any business owner to gain the benefit of expert strategic thinking—on demand.

Our Mission

We believe every business owner deserves clarity, profit, and peace. AskStephen.ai was designed to be your digital advisor—combining data, automation, and proven strategy frameworks to help you make decisions that grow your company sustainably.

Our Vision

To create a generation of small and mid-sized business leaders who run thriving, system-driven organizations that serve their people, their clients, and their communities.
