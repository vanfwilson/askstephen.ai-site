SIGN UP PAGE

The signup.html page requires an opt-in form to be added.

You can use any autoresponder service you like. i.e.Aweber or GetResponse or any other.

Create an opt-in form with your chosen autoresponder or email service and replace the current form on the signup.html page.

See form for editing between -- REPLACE THE FORM BELOW WITH YOUR OWN OPT_IN FORM -- and -- REPLACE THE FORM ABOVE WITH YOUR OWN OPT_IN FORM --

Alternatively you could replace the signup.html page completely with one from your autoresponder or email service app. Many email services now offer that option.

In any case you do need to set the redirect or final destination page to the thankyou page i.e. https://yoursite.com/thankyou.html. This is the page that leads will be sent to after they opt-in.
