Wisdom in Action

The foundation of true leadership begins with wisdom and humility. At AskStephen.ai, we believe technology should serve people — not replace them — and that all great decisions start with divine wisdom.

“If any of you lacks wisdom, you should ask God, who gives generously to all without finding fault.” — James 1:5

Our founders, Stephen Wright and Dr. Van Wilson, combine years of business, data science, and ministry experience to guide leaders in faith-based growth. Stephen regularly leads Bible studies in his home, while Dr. Wilson’s doctoral work in divinity bridges faith and science — showing how data validates Biblical truths.

Faith and Data: A Balanced Approach

Faith and data are not opposites — they are partners. Through AskStephen.ai, leaders can apply data science to reveal what Scripture has always taught: principles of stewardship, order, and excellence.

  • Integrity builds sustainable growth (Proverbs 10:9)
  • Stewardship multiplies blessings (Luke 16:10–12)
  • Service strengthens influence (Matthew 23:11)

Faith, Mentorship & Prayer

Both Stephen Wright and Dr. Van Wilson are available to pray with or for clients in need — for healing, prosperity, clarity, and courage. We believe prayer and partnership are essential for transformation.

“Two are better than one, because they have a good return for their labor.” — Ecclesiastes 4:9

As leadership mentors, we draw inspiration from voices like John Maxwell, who teaches that “leadership is influence,” and Myron Golden, who reminds us that “wealth is a tool for service.” These lessons align perfectly with Biblical stewardship and our mission to build businesses that honor God.
