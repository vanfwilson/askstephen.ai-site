Privacy Policy

We take your privacy seriously. This page explains what information we collect, how we use it, and how we protect it.

1. Who We Are

AskStephen.ai is operated by New View Innovation Financial, LLC in partnership with 3A Automation Authority. Together, we provide consulting, education, AI-driven SaaS tools, and mastermind programs for business executives.

2. Information We Collect

We collect the following types of information when you use our website or services:

  • Personal information (name, email, phone, company) through forms and purchases.
  • Billing information for subscription payments via WooCommerce or Stripe.
  • Usage data from our SaaS application, including analytics and preferences.
  • Cookies and tracking data to improve website performance and personalization.

3. How We Use Your Information

  • To deliver, improve, and personalize our SaaS tools and consulting services.
  • To communicate with you regarding updates, offers, and newsletters (with your consent).
  • To process orders, subscriptions, and payments securely.
  • To comply with legal and regulatory obligations.

4. Sharing Your Information

We do not sell or rent your personal information. We may share limited data with trusted partners and vendors who assist in running our services (such as WooCommerce, Fluent Forms, or analytics tools). These partners are bound by confidentiality and data protection agreements.

5. Cookies and Analytics

We use cookies to enhance your browsing experience and track usage analytics through services such as Google Analytics. You can control or delete cookies through your browser settings at any time.

6. Data Retention and Security

We retain personal data only as long as necessary to provide our services or comply with legal obligations. Your data is stored securely with encryption and access controls. We review security measures regularly to maintain compliance with GDPR and CCPA standards.

7. Your Rights

You have the right to access, correct, or delete your personal information. To make a request, please contact us at info@askstephen.ai. You may also unsubscribe from marketing communications at any time using the link in our emails.

8. Policy Updates

We may update this policy periodically to reflect changes in technology, regulations, or business practices. The date of the latest revision will always appear at the bottom of this page.
